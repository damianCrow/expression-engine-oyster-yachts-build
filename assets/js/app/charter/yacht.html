Oyster 885

Lush

The Oyster 885 Lush is one of the newest models to be designed by Oyster. Among her many attractions are the spacious cockpit perfect for outside dining and relaxation on charter with open deck areas, and a large flip-down bathing platform. The saloon is accessed from the cockpit via a wide glass sliding door. The interior joinery features a light stained teak to give a striking modern effect. The guest accommodation benefits from tall triple seascape windows that give these rooms a bright and spacious feel. The aft cabin offers a queen sized bed, desk and couch. Forward of the master cabin are two guest double cabins, one either side of the corridor. Each has en-suite WCs and showers and a hanging locker with ample drawers under the berths. A fourth guest cabin is forward of the saloon. All cabins have a private ensuite head and shower and fully integrated entertainment system with Apple TV.

The crew

Captain Tim Beebe

Captain Tim Beebe grew up in Indonesia and spent much of his youth sailing Dinghies and Hobie Cats off the coast of Java. From Indonesia he returned to England to read a degree in Business Studies, however, the lure of sailing and an outdoor lifestyle soon saw him on board a yacht heading to the Caribbean. Since then he has logged over 70,000 miles, completed eight Atlantic crossings and has a Commercially Endorsed Yacht Master ticket. Tim is also an experienced diver, with a Padi Divemaster qualification. Originally from the UK, Tim has worked on a variety of sailing yachts, including 4 Oysters, throughout the Caribbean, Mediterranean, East Coastof the US and in the UK.

Chef Anne Last Name

Anne your chef is from New Zealand and after graduating from University with a bachelor of arts, left New Zealand to travel the world. In 2005 she discovered her passion for the sea and has been working on luxury yachts since then. In addition to studying cookery at the prestigious Ashburton chefs academy Anne has attended various short courses and specialty workshops in Mediterranean and Asian cuisine. Anne loves to experiment with fresh local produce creating beautiful dishes that entertain and delight her guests

Chief Mate Alvie Last Name

Alvie the mate brings a great deal of sea miles to the team. He most recently completed a circumnavigation with the Oyster World Rally. As well as holding his Yachtmaster Ocean certificate Alvie is a keen water sports enthusiast and will look forward to taking you wake boarding, water skiing, snorkelling , fishing and much more. Originally from Madrid, Alvie has travelled extensively and is fluent in three languages.
