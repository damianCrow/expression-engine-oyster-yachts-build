Welcome to
Oyster Charter

A holiday with Oyster Charter is an opportunity to sample life on board some of the most luxurious yachts afloat and your passport to the ultimate cruising experience. We have a range of yachts from 56 to 125 feet which are all individually owned and impeccably maintained. Every Oyster yacht is built with blue water live-aboard cruising in mind. Great sailing performance, sea keeping, spacious cockpits for that all-important on-board dining experience, and comfortable spacious interior layouts come as standard.

Oyster Charter specialises in providing a bespoke service for both Owners and Charter guests. All Oyster Charter yachts are operated by a professional crew, allowing you to enjoy your holiday. Whether you are an experienced yachtsman or complete novice your Oyster charter will be at your pace, adventurous sailing or lazy days in a secluded anchorage, the choice is yours. Relax, unwind and soak up the lifestyle on board.
