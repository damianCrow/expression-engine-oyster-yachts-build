Welcome to
Oyster Brokerage

Established in 1984 to offer Oyster owners a specialised brokerage service when they wished to offer their yachts for sale, Oyster Brokerage has now grown to become an integral part of the Oyster customer experience.

Our dedicated team of international yacht brokers has extensive knowledge of every Oyster built, with unique access to the history, original build files, photographic library, as well as to the original build yards. Comprehensive and detailed sales specifications are therefore guaranteed, a reassuring advantage for both purchaser and vendor.

Internationally recognised as the pre-owned Oyster specialists, it is not surprising that we are responsible for selling the vast majority of Oyster yachts that change hands each year. We have a strong presence at the most prestigious international boat shows as well as organizing our own spring boat show in Ipswich, and autumn boat show in Southampton. Of course, visitors are always welcome at our offices in Ipswich, Palma de Mallorca, or Newport, Rhode Island, USA, where we always have a superb selection of pre-owned Oysters available for inspection at any convenient time.
