Introducing the
new Oyster fleets

All Oyster yachts are unique personal yachts, built to order, working closely with you during the build. We offer a range of yachts from 47ft to 118ft with more customisation available on the larger yachts. The hull shapes, deck and sail layouts have been refined from thousands of miles of deep ocean experience with over a thousand yachts over more than 40 years.

Comfortable, stylish and beautifully engineered, your Oyster will take you sailing safely anywhere your dreams desire.
