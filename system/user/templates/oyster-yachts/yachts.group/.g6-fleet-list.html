G6 Fleet

First developed with the Oyster 885 hull in 2011, the 'G6’ fleet now offers seven models from the new Oyster 565 and Oyster 595 to the Oyster 118.

The ‘G6’ fleet all have benefitted from Oyster's experience in the Superyacht sector over 24m. Extensively tank-tested to refine the hull shapes, the twin rudders and the simple yet powerful rig configurations.

They all feature powerful hulls, flush decks, sleek styling and flexibility in the interior design.
