Introducing the
new Oyster fleets

We offer a range of yachts from 47ft to 118ft. In each there is a focus on the owner - the master suite proportions, the social separation of crew and guest living spaces in the larger yachts, we focus on how the interior works as how the yacht sails. Built in two shipyards - one for the larger yachts where more customisation is possible and the other personalising the smaller yachts - each Oyster is unique. We encourage you to visit regularly during the build.
