Forgot Username?

If you've forgotten your username, just enter your email address you registered with and you will receive an email informing you of your username.

Forgot Password?

If you've forgotten your password, just enter your email address you registered with and you will receive an email with instructions for resetting your password.

About This Page

The "Forgot Username / Password" page allows users to have their username mailed to them or their password reset, in the event they forget it.

It makes use of the following tags:

  • User:Forgot_Username to allow users have their username mailed to them.
  • User:Forgot_Password to allow users reset their account password.
